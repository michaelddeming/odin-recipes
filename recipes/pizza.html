Pizza

Description

Cheese pizza is the epitome of simplicity, yet its allure is undeniable. A crispy, golden crust serves as the foundation for a bubbling blanket of mozzarella, perfectly melted and slightly browned at the edges. The tangy tomato sauce provides a subtle contrast to the richness of the cheese, with hints of garlic and herbs mingling in each bite. It’s the kind of pizza that doesn’t need elaborate toppings to impress; the balance of flavors and textures speaks for itself. Whether enjoyed fresh from the oven or as a cold slice for breakfast, cheese pizza is a universal comfort food that satisfies every craving.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomoato sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Prepare dough: Mix flour, yeast, water, salt; let it rise.
  3. Roll out dough: Shape it into a flat circle.
  4. Add sauce: Spread tomato sauce evenly on the dough.
  5. Add cheese: Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  6. Season: Add salt, oregano, and optional garlic.
  7. Bake: Bake for 10-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  8. Cool and serve: Let cool slightly, then slice and serve.
